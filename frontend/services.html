Our Services

Comprehensive Estimation & Cost Control.

General Construction Estimation

We provide full structural and architectural estimations. By accurately calculating the quantity of concrete, rebar, masonry, framing, and finishes, we ensure that your base project costs are tightly managed.

  • Material takeoffs
  • Labor hour estimation
  • Equipment cost forecasting

MEP Estimation (Mechanical, Electrical, Plumbing)

MEP trades are often the most complex and expensive parts of a building project. We specialize in providing granular details for:

  • Mechanical: HVAC ducts, piping, insulation, and equipment costs.
  • Electrical: Wiring, conduit, lighting fixtures, switchgear, and low-voltage systems.
  • Plumbing: Pipe lengths, fixtures, valves, and drainage systems.

BIM and Cost Modeling Services

Move beyond 2D plans with our advanced BIM (Building Information Modeling) services. We create 5D cost models that link directly to 3D designs, allowing you to visualize costs instantly as designs change and foresee potential clashes before they occur on-site.

Bill of Quantities (BOQ) Preparation

A detailed BOQ is crucial for fair bidding. We prepare comprehensive BOQs broken down by trade, ensuring transparent and itemized lists of all materials and labor required for the project.
